## **Service Tiers (Reorganized)**

### **Tier 1: Simple & Fast** 🌐
- **For:** Startups, solopreneurs, small businesses
- **What:** Landing pages, brochure websites, portfolio sites
- **Timeline:** 2-4 weeks
- **Price:** $8k - $20k
- **Examples:** Landing Page Project
- **Outcome:** Get online quickly with modern, SEO-friendly presence

### **Tier 2: Custom Internal Systems** 👥
- **For:** Small and mid-size businesses with manual processes
- **What:** HR, payroll, inventory, workflow automation
- **Timeline:** 2-6 months
- **Price:** $30k - $150k
- **Examples:** HR Management System, Payroll System
- **Outcome:** Replace scattered tools with unified platform, reduce manual work 50-80%

### **Tier 3: SaaS & Product Development** 🚀
- **For:** Startups and growth-stage companies
- **What:** Build software product from MVP to scale
- **Timeline:** 3-6 months (MVP) + ongoing
- **Price:** $80k - $250k (MVP)
- **Examples:** Payment Settlement Platform, E-commerce Search, Order Fulfillment
- **Outcome:** Market-ready product with scalable architecture

### **Tier 4: Enterprise Scale & Integration** 🏗️
- **For:** Established companies, mission-critical systems
- **What:** Microservices, modernization, compliance automation
- **Timeline:** 3-12 months
- **Price:** $150k - $500k+
- **Examples:** Microservices Banking, Event Streaming, Real-time Analytics
- **Outcome:** 99.99% SLA, sub-100ms latency, compliance-ready

### **Tier 5: Security & Fraud Prevention** 🔐
- **For:** Any platform handling transactions/sensitive data
- **What:** Fraud detection, API security, breach detection, compliance
- **Timeline:** Varies
- **Price:** Varies
- **Examples:** Fraud Detection, API Attack Detection, Breach Forensics, KYC/AML
- **Outcome:** Prevent losses, protect data, maintain customer trust

### **Tier 6: Ongoing Support & Leadership** 👨‍💼
- **For:** Teams at any stage
- **What:** Fractional CTO, code review, hiring, guidance
- **Timeline:** 3-12 months or ongoing
- **Price:** $2k - $20k/month
- **Examples:** Works with any service above
- **Outcome:** Expert direction, reduced risk, faster decisions
